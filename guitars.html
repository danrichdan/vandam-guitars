Here are some examples of Van Dam Guitar's work. We should have five new ones available this fall, one SGT and 3 other models. SGT Sunset Fireburst - Sold

The SGT Sunset Fireburst guitar is a solid body electric guitar with Tele-style pickups. The body is Alder, the neck is Maple with a Bocote fretboard. The neck is thick and comfortable and has 22 frets. The body color is a combination of oranges and reds.

Specifications

    Alder
  • Control Cavity Cover:
    Bocote
  • Body Contours:
    Belly, Forearm, Cutout
  • Finish:
    Environmentally friendly water based polyurethane
  • Color:
    Sunset Fireburst
  • Neck Wood:
    Quartersawn Maple
  • Neck Finish:
    Danish Oil
  • Fretboard Material:
    Bocote
  • Inlays:
    Abalone, White Side Dots
  • Frets:
    22, Stainless Steel, 6105
  • Nut:
    Bone
  • Scale Length:
    25.5
  • Fretboard Radius:
    12 in
  • Tuners:
    Hip Shot Locking Tuners
  • Bridge:
    Gotoh Tele Bridge, String through
  • Pickups:
    Fralin Telecaster Pickup Set
  • Hardware:
    Chrome
  • Strings:
    10-46

SGT Ocean Turquoiseburst - Sold

The SGT Ocean Turquoiseburst guitar is a solid body electric guitar with Tele-style pickups. The body is African Mahogany and has a Quilted Maple top, the neck is Maple with a Ziricote fretboard. The neck is thick and comfortable and has 22 frets. The body color is a combination of greens and blues.
